In september of 2021, I wanted to reinvigerate myself and my relationship with photography. If you'll recall
I did a similar challenge the [Geology Series](/blog/geology-series) in 2020 to try and beat the boredom of
isolation. This challenge was geared at color exploration. I feel fairly comfortable in the realm of black
and white, but when color is part of the equation I feel uncertain.

I tried to challenge myself.
